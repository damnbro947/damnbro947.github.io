Recycling, asphalt and industrial services

Alex Fraser is Australia’s leading producer of sustainable materials for the civil construction industry. We produce high quality products, including aggregates, asphalt, road base and sand at seven facilities in Melbourne and Brisbane. We focus on excellence in delivery reliability and provide measurable improvements in environmental outcomes.
